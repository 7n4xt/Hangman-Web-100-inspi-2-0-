How to Play

  • You have 6 attempts to save the engine
  • Each wrong letter costs 1 attempt
  • Guessing the wrong word costs 2 attempts
  • Words are related to engines and mechanics

Winning & Losing

  • Win by saving the a engine through correct guesses
  • Each engine brings a harder challenge
  • Lose if you run out of attempts
  • Don't let the engine fall from the hoist!
